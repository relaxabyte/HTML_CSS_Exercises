Haiku Generator

A Haiku is a very short form of Japanese poetry. It is traditionally a three line observation that follows a pattern of 5, 7, 5 syllables per line respectively and generally do not rhyme.

Given any list of words in syllibification form, generate a random Haiku. It does not need to make grammatical sense, however it must follow the 3 line, 5, 7, 5 syllable pattern
